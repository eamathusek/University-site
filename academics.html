ACADEMICS

We value your education

The International Fleet has turned out some of the most brilliant minds in history. Our educational system is above reproach. Mazer Rackham's strategies and tactics have helped fuel our curriculum, as well as information on utilizing the latest in military technology.

Like a typical school on Earth, children take basic classes for mathematics and other studies. The classes also emphasize military topics, such as battle strategy and military history. If one chose, they can take a class in self defense. Students are allowed to do various simulator games, in the game room and on their personal Desks. The most significant of the games is the Mind Game, or the Fantasy Game. Its primary function is not to learn strategy and tactics, but to evaluate the psychology of the player.

The children dwell in a highly structured environment, and are chosen specifically for their special abilities to function well within the circumstances of Battle School. As a result, discipline remains high, yet creativity is mostly suppressed.
