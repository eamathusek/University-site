History

How we came to be

Creation

The station that would become Battle School was originally constructed before 100 AX as a permanent weigh station between Earth and Luna.

Formation into Battle School

Some time after Mazer Rackham was sent on his life-extending time-dilated flight, he took himself hostage, seizing control of his ship from the International Fleet and making clear he would not allow it to make the return trip until his handlers met his demands. With the help of an ambitious young Hyrum Graff, he successfully reformed the military bureaucracy, giving Graff a free hand to organize and administrate his Battle School for prodigies that would train them to be commanders for the Third Invasion The weigh station was then commandeered by the Fleet and turned into Battle School.

Fleet School

After the Third Formic War had ended and Battle School lost its purpose in training children for war, it was converted into Fleet School, administrated by the Ministry of Colonization. It trained children to be explorers or governors in the new colonies as a part of the Dispersal Project.
